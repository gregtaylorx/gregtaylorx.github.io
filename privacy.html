VisaStay Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 3, 2026

VisaStay ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the VisaStay mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use VisaStay:

  • Account information: Your email address and password when you create an account.
  • Profile information: Your passport country, passport expiry date (optional), and home currency, which you provide to enable visa calculations and expiry alerts.
  • Trip data: Country codes, city names, travel dates, trip names, trip notes, intent status (e.g. planning, visa applied), and passport country for trips you add to the app.
  • Notification preferences: Your notification rules including the email address(es) you choose to receive visa expiry alerts, and whether you have enabled push or email notifications.
  • Subscription information: Whether you hold a free or Pro subscription. Payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play or the Apple App Store — we never see or store payment card details.

We do not collect your precise location, contacts, photos, microphone access, or any other device data beyond what is listed above.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to provide the VisaStay service, including:

  • Calculating visa duration and days remaining for your trips
  • Displaying Schengen 90/180-day rolling window status
  • Storing your trip history so you can track past and future travel
  • Displaying relevant visa rules based on your passport country
  • Sending push and email notifications about upcoming visa expiry dates and passport expiry dates
  • Authenticating your account and keeping it secure
  • Managing your Pro subscription entitlement

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than providing the VisaStay service.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider. All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS and at rest using industry-standard encryption. Supabase's privacy policy can be found at supabase.com/privacy.

A local encrypted cache of your trip data is stored on your device to enable offline access. This cache is cleared when you sign out.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Supabase: Our database provider processes your data solely to store and retrieve it on our behalf.
  • RevenueCat: We use RevenueCat to manage Pro subscription entitlements. RevenueCat receives your app store user identifier and subscription status. RevenueCat's privacy policy is at revenuecat.com/privacy.
  • Resend: We use Resend to deliver email notifications. Resend receives the destination email address and the notification content you have configured. Resend's privacy policy is at resend.com/legal/privacy-policy.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data in your profile
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your trip data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below.

6. Account Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through the Profile screen in the app, or by contacting us at the email address below. Upon deletion, all your personal information, profile data, trip history, and notification settings will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

You can also request deletion directly at: gregtaylorx.github.io/delete-account.html

7. Children's Privacy and Age Ratings

VisaStay is intended for use by adults and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately and we will delete it promptly.

In compliance with the Texas App Store Accountability Act (SB 2420, effective January 1, 2026) and similar applicable laws:

  • VisaStay is rated Everyone / 4+ across all app stores. The app contains no violence, adult content, gambling, or other age-restricted material.
  • We provide an accurate age rating to each app store marketplace through which VisaStay is distributed.
  • If an app store provides us with age or parental-consent data in connection with a user's account, we use that information solely for compliance purposes, transmit it using industry-standard encryption, and delete it after use. We do not retain or use it for any other purpose.
  • Parents or guardians who believe a minor is using VisaStay may contact us to request review or deletion of the account.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all personal data is removed within 30 days. Anonymised, non-identifiable aggregate data (such as total number of countries in the database) may be retained indefinitely for service improvement purposes.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Disclaimer

Visa rules and duration information provided by VisaStay is for informational purposes only. It may not reflect the most current regulations. Always verify visa requirements with official government sources or your country's embassy before travelling. VisaStay is not responsible for any consequences arising from reliance on information provided by the app.
